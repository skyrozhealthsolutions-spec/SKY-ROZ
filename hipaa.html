HIPAA Compliance

At SKY ROZ, we prioritize your patients' privacy and data security. Our medical billing solutions strictly adhere to HIPAA regulations, ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of protected health information (PHI).

  • ✔ Data Security: Advanced encryption and secure transmission protocols protect sensitive information.
  • ✔ Access Control: Strict authentication and authorization measures to restrict data access.
  • ✔ Confidentiality: Employee training and compliance policies uphold patient privacy at all times.
  • ✔ Compliance Audits: Regular audits ensure ongoing adherence to HIPAA standards.
  • ✔ Business Associate Agreement: We sign BAA agreements to ensure mutual responsibility for data protection.

Trust SKY ROZ to handle your billing with the highest standards of security and professionalism.
